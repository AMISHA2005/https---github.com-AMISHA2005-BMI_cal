Welcome to BMI Calculator! This tool helps you estimate your Body Mass Index (BMI),thus providing insight into your body's composition based on your weight and height. Understanding your BMI can be a valuable indicator of your overall health.

Before calculating, please fill in the required information such as your gender, age, weight, and height in the given fields. You can also indicate your activity level and weight-related goals using the provided checkboxes and radio buttons.
